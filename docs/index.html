Why this exists

Opening training shouldn't cost a subscription

Most chess opening trainers lock their best features behind a monthly fee. ChessOpenings was built on a simple conviction: learning the Italian Game, the Sicilian or the Queen's Gambit should be free for everyone — the openings themselves are centuries old and belong to all of us.

So this app does one thing, does it well, and asks for nothing back. Every opening, every line, every feature — free on day one, free forever. The full source code is public under the GPL-3.0 license, which legally guarantees it can never be turned into a paid, closed product. Not even by its author.

It also runs entirely offline. No servers, no tracking, no data leaving your computer. Train on a plane, in a park, anywhere.

Features

Everything you need to master your repertoire

📖

Learn mode

Step through every move with plain-language explanations of the ideas behind it.

♟️

Practice mode

Play the moves yourself. The opponent replies automatically — and wrong moves show you which piece punishes them.

👑

80+ openings

From beginner classics to the Najdorf — plus fun gambits like the Bongcloud. Each with multiple lines.

🔥

Daily streaks

Build a training habit with streak tracking and a weekly calendar of your progress.

⭐

Favorites & filters

Star your repertoire, filter by difficulty or color, and find any opening instantly.

🌙

Polished experience

Dark and light themes, subtle sounds, keyboard shortcuts, progress saved per opening.
