Why this exists

Opening training shouldn't cost a subscription

Most opening trainers lock their best features behind a monthly fee. But the Italian Game, the Sicilian and the Queen's Gambit are centuries old — they belong to everyone. ChessOpenings was built on that conviction: one focused tool, done well, free on day one and free forever.

It runs entirely offline. No servers, no tracking, no data ever leaving your computer. And the full source code is public under the GPL-3.0 license — which legally guarantees that no one can ever turn this into a paid, closed product. Not even its author.
